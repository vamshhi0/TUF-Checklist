## 🧠 Overview  

A **production-ready DSA tracking system** built to eliminate outdated preparation methods and replace them with a **structured, digital, and execution-driven workflow**.

This platform is not just a checklist — it's a **discipline engine**.

> ⚡ Plan less. Execute more. Track everything.

---

## ❌ The Problem  

Most aspirants:
- Download DSA sheets and never complete them  
- Print PDFs and lose track midway  
- Start with motivation but fail at consistency  

---

## ✅ The Solution  

A **minimal, powerful, and distraction-free system** that helps you:

- 📊 Track progress visually  
- 🎯 Stay accountable daily  
- 🔁 Build long-term discipline  
- 🚀 Actually *finish* your DSA roadmap
